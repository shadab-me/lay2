Our Departments

Specialized Care for Better Recovery

From routine diagnostics to complex surgeries, we deliver tailored medical care for Amroha's community needs.

Advanced Surgical Unit

Laparoscopic and open surgeries performed with precision using German-made equipment. Specializing in hernia repair, gallbladder removal, and appendectomies with faster recovery and minimal scarring.

Mother & Child Health

Safe deliveries, C-sections, and neonatal care in hygienic labor suites. Expert care for high-risk pregnancies, prenatal care, and laparoscopic hysterectomies under expert supervision.

24/7 Trauma Center

Fully-equipped emergency unit with ambulance services across Amroha district. Immediate response for critical care needs with experienced trauma team available round the clock.

In-House Laboratory

Rapid blood tests, ultrasound scans, and digital X-ray reports for accurate diagnosis. State-of-the-art equipment ensuring precise results with quick turnaround times.

Women's Wellness Programs

Comprehensive women's health services including gynecological surgeries, preventive health screenings, and specialized care for all stages of womanhood.

Preventive Healthcare

Regular health check-ups, vaccinations, and lifestyle counseling. Our preventive care programs help maintain optimal health and catch potential issues early.

Orthopedics & Joint Care

Expert treatment for bone and joint conditions, sports injuries, and arthritis. Specialized care for fractures and joint replacements with modern rehabilitation facilities.

Child Healthcare

Comprehensive pediatric services including vaccinations, growth monitoring, and treatment of childhood illnesses. Special focus on newborn care and developmental assessment.

Dental Services

Complete dental care including routine check-ups, fillings, root canals, and cosmetic dentistry. Advanced treatments with modern dental equipment and experienced specialists.

Heart Care Center

Comprehensive cardiac care including ECG, stress tests, and heart disease management. Focus on preventive cardiology and lifestyle modifications for heart health.

Neurology Services

Expert care for neurological disorders, headaches, and spine problems. Advanced diagnostic facilities including EEG and nerve conduction studies.

Mental Wellness

Confidential counseling services, stress management, and treatment for mental health conditions. Holistic approach to emotional and psychological well-being.
